Tab Wrapper Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Tab Wrapper collects tab titles and URLs to categorize them using AI. This data is sent to our backend server for processing only during the organization operation.

Data Storage

We do not store, sell, or share your tab data with third parties. All data is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after categorization.

Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • tabs - To read tab titles and URLs for categorization
  • tabGroups - To create and manage tab groups
  • storage - To save your preferences locally
  • windows - To access window information
